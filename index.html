    Welcome to MageObsidian¶

    A modern, groundbreaking frontend stack for Magento. MageObsidian redefines frontend development by integrating Vite, TailwindCSS, and Vue.js on top of Magento's native layouts, blocks, and templates — for a faster, friendlier workflow.
